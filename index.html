About Me

My name is Donna Lee, and I am excited to showcase my work to YOU! I grew up in a small town in Tennessee and moved to New York City to attend New York University. I earned my Bachelor's of Science degree in Hospitality management with hopes of running a prominent hotel or restaurant. I built my experience around different types of restaurant concepts and grew to love the industry, but I have always wanted to understand the business side of the industry. I pivoted my career to Human Resources, having worked with Claus Meyer, Din Tai Fung, and Mendocino Farms. With years of experience in both operations and administrative added to my resume, I wanted to take on a new challenge.

My decision to attend coding bootcamp was a pivotable moment, but I knew that the skills and knowledge I obtained through coding bootcamp will allow we to better understand one's needs, whether it is for employees, guests, or the company from a different angle foreign to me before.
